WELCOME TO COLLEGE DASHBOARD

Sinhgad institute of management. Sinhgad Institute of Management (MCA), Pune is one of the top 50 MCA institutes in India. The Master of Computer Application Program has started in 1996 under the umbrella of Sinhgad Technical Education Society with the objective of providing high quality & holistic education in the field of Computer Application. It is a full time three years program affiliated to university of Pune, Approved by AICTE, New Delihi, Government of India & Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Government of Maharashtra. Over these years the Institute has earned number one position in Pune. This is the first NBA accredited MCA private institute in Maharashtra. SIOM campus enjoys lush green surroundings, panoramic views conductive for a perfect learning environment. Our institute is well equipped with state-of-art infrastructure well blended with technology. Our institute has a strong track record of merit rank holders in university of Pune since its inception. college life is known as one of the most memorable years of one's life.
